

# 🚍 NamasteRoute

### AI-Powered Smart Public Transit Assistant

NamasteRoute is an intelligent public transit planning platform that helps commuters choose the safest, smartest, and most sustainable travel routes.

Instead of showing only travel time and cost, NamasteRoute analyzes multiple factors such as safety, crowd levels, carbon impact, convenience, and route reliability to recommend the best travel option.

---

## 🌟 Features

### 🤖 AI Route Recommendations

Get intelligent route suggestions based on:

* Travel Time
* Cost
* Safety Score
* Carbon Impact
* Transfer Count
* Convenience Score

The AI explains why a particular route is recommended.

---

### 🛡️ Safety Intelligence

Each route includes:

* Safety Score
* Crowd Risk
* Night Travel Risk
* Community Safety Insights

Users can enable **Women Safe Mode** to prioritize safer travel options.
